About

Django Lessons is a weekly screencast about Django Web Framework and related ecosystem. The screencasts are short and focus on one technique so you can quickly move on to applying it to your own project. The topics target the intermediate Django developer, but beginners and experts will get something out of it as well.

Author

My name is Eugen. I graduated in 2007 Bachelor of Computer Science (5 years course). From 2007 to 2018 I worked for couple of IT companies in C, Ruby and Python (and hundreds of other tools and technologies).

In 2019, I finally had enough courage, experience and capital to start something on my own. In december 2019 I registered, here in Berlin, Germany, one-man business (Einzelunternehmen in German).

Besides django lessons, I am working on Papermerge as well. Papermerge is an open source document management system designed for scanned documents. Here is an introductory screencast about the project.
